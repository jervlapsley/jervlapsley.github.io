I love creating, but I'm always torn between creating content and living in the moment.

I've come to notice that many content creators spend most time seeking the perfect shot that they  do not appear to be truly enjoying themselves -- I refuse to be like that.

At the same time, I often look back and regret not documenting an event I experienced, whether for own memories or to share with others. 

My solution to this is:

**Keep. It. Raw.** 

The cameras can keep rolling, but my focus stays 100% on the moment, the magic can be made later in production.
