I love creating, but I'm always torn between creating content and living in the moment.

I've come to notice that many content creators spend most time seeking the perfect shot and do not appear to be truly enjoying them selves -- I refuse to be like that.

I often look back and regret not documenting an event I experienced, whether for memories or sharing with others. 

My solutuion to this is:
Keep. It. RAW. 

Cameras rolling, but my focus is 100% on the moment, the magic will be made in production.
